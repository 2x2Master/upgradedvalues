What is UltimateValues?

UltimateValues is a test that measures political values and leanings on different issues. Take the test and find out where you stand!

What are the values?

There are nine independent axes, where each has two opposing values assigned to them. They are:

Progress
This axis measures political views on progress and cultures. Progress tends to favour putting rapid change above the traditions of the past, Traditional tends to prefer preserving important traditions over the ages.

Identity
This axis measures views on identity and minorities. Inclusion tends to favour ideas of equality, representation and reperation, Normatality tends to prefer recognition of groups as more common or the standard, and at the extreme, supremacy over other groups.

Production
This axis measures views on production, automation and technology. Regression tends to favour returning to a previous era of production, Automation tends to prefer new technology being used to improve production.
